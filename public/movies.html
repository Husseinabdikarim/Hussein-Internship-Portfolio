The Best Movies According to Hussein

My Top 3 Movies in 2020

The Old Guard

"The Old Guard" is an action-fantasy film about a group of immortal mercenaries led by Andy (Charlize Theron). When a new immortal, Nile (KiKi Layne), emerges, they must protect her and face a ruthless pharmaceutical executive who wants to exploit their powers. Themes of immortality, friendship, and sacrifice unfold in a thrilling battle between good and evil.
This movie captivates with its spectacular action sequences and explores the complex themes of immortality and friendship, resulting in an engrossing and thought-provoking movie. A must watch movie for sure.

Extraction

"Extraction" (2020) is an action-thriller film where mercenary Tyler Rake (Chris Hemsworth) is hired to rescue Ovi, the kidnapped son of a drug lord, in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Facing a dangerous and corrupt underworld, Tyler embarks on a high-stakes mission filled with intense action sequences and gripping fight scenes. As the rescue mission unfolds, Tyler forms a bond with Ovi, adding a personal dimension to the adrenaline-pumping journey. The film showcases Chris Hemsworth's action prowess and delivers a thrilling experience for audiences.
This movie has amazing action scenes that electrify the screen, delivering a pulse-pounding experience that keeps you on the edge of your seat throughout.
This movie boasts jaw-dropping action sequences that elevate the film's intensity, making it an adrenaline-fueled and riveting cinematic experience.

Project Power

"Project Power" is a 2020 sci-fi action film set in New Orleans, where a mysterious new drug grants its users superpowers for five minutes. As the drug spreads, a former soldier (played by Jamie Foxx), seeking to rescue his kidnapped daughter, teams up with a local cop (Joseph Gordon-Levitt) and a teenage dealer (Dominique Fishback). Together, they navigate the dangerous world of superpowered criminals and uncover a sinister conspiracy behind the drug's creation.
The movie combines intense action, thrilling superpower sequences, and thought-provoking themes of ethics and power, creating an engaging and fast-paced cinematic experience.
